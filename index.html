About Me

My name is Madhura and I am from Kovilpatti. I have completed my schoolings at Edustar International school which is in my hometown. I am one of the members in the family of 4, mom, dad, brother and I. Now I am an 19 years old undergraduate engineering student pursuing Electronics Communication and Engineering course at sri krishna college of engineering and technology, Coimbatore.

Well, I am a keen writer and I love songs so that I cheer me up each time I feel low with these songs. In the same way I do listen to a lot of podcasts. I am a more fun loving person and I enjoy talking to myself as it is the only possible way of improving myself each day. I am here to improve my skills and knowledge which is going to help me in my later life, which mainly includes the choosing of right path for my career.

As of now I have ideas like either appearing for entrance exams to pursue MBA or entering into the core jobs. So finally I hope either by end or beginning of the third year I will be able to choose one right path to accomplish my future goals in my later life. I become upset for small things but in the same way I become the happiest for even the small good thing happening around me.

The only thing that keeps me going is that " I have already won this life by coming into this world in the first place but I should ensure that my first win has not ever been due to luck".
